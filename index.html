The IWW is a union for all workers — One Big Union!
Join the Maple City General Membership Branch today!


As long as you are a worker – not an employer – you can join the IWW. Members of other unions (except officers), students, retirees, the unemployed, the self-employed, those in informal professions, and those unable to work may also join. Learn why you should join the IWW.

Interested in organizing? Contact us today!


Having trouble at work? Want to organize with your coworkers against your boss? Contact the IWW today and meet with experienced IWW organizers, attend an organizer training, and, with the mentorship and guidance of other Wobblies, build a union with your fellow workers at your job and in your industry. Get in touch with us by emailing organize@iww.nyc. Read Weakening the Dam to get a short primer on what an organzing campaign with the IWW could look like.

Maple City General Membership Branch meetings are held the second Friday of each month from 6pm-8pm.
